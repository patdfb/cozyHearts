---

## The Problem

Elderly loneliness is a big problem and a major health concern in today’s society. Not only does it affect someone’s mental health but it can also be a toll on their physical health. The lack of accessible tools to connect them with opportunities, such as community resources and services available nearby, contributes to social isolation.

---

## Our Solution

An easy-to-use mobile app that connects elderly residents within the same community based on shared interests, encouraging social interaction and active participation. The app can also link users with nearby services, tending to their needs.

The app allows users to create a simple profile containing basic information and personal interests. Profile setup can be done with the help of family members or caregivers to ensure accessibility.

Users are matched with local groups according to common interests and geographic location. Activities available on the platform come from two sources: initiatives created by government or municipality entities, and activities created by the groups themselves.

Groups created within the app can contact directly the nearby community centers to request information about the meetings or request a meeting at government facilities, which provide a safe place to meet.
